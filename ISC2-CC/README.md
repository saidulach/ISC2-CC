## Overview

The Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) is an entry-level certification by (ISC)Â² that validates your foundational knowledge and skills in cybersecurity. This globally recognized credential is ANAB accredited and aligns with ISO/IEC 17024, making it an excellent starting point for your cybersecurity career.

### Exam Format
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Questions**: 100 multiple-choice questions
- **Passing Score**: 700 out of 1000 points
- **Question Types**: Knowledge-based and scenario-based
- **Language**: English only
- **Testing Options**: Online proctored or testing center

## Exam Blueprint

The exam covers five essential domains with the following approximate weights:

| Domain | Weight | Key Focus Areas |

| Access Control Concepts | 22% | Authentication, authorization, accountability |

| Security Operations | 18% | Data security, monitoring, system hardening |
